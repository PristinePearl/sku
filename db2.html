üìò Inventory Terminology

Physical Stock

Total on-hand quantity across all depots as per latest snapshot.

Usable Stock

Available stock after excluding short-expiry inventory.

Program Requirement

Monthly sales planning requirement derived from prior-year sales with 30%.

OSH Requirement

Monthly Stock requirement defined by Operational Sales Head.

MOQ

Minimum Order Quantity mandated by the manufacturer.

Usable Stock

$$\text{Usable Stock} = \text{Physical Stock} - \text{Short Expiry Qty}$$

Daily Average Sales

$$\text{Daily Avg Sales} = \frac{\text{Program Requirement}}{30}$$

Stock Coverage (Days)

$$\text{Coverage Days} = \frac{\text{Usable Stock}}{\text{Daily Avg Sales}}$$

$$\text{Base Qty} = (\text{Lead Time} + \text{Safety Stock}) \times \text{Daily Avg Sales}$$ $$\text{Suggested Order} = \max(\text{Base Qty} - \text{Usable Stock} - \text{Open PO}, 0)$$
